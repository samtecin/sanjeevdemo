About SanjeevDemo

Certified Yoga Instructor with over 10 years of experience helping students achieve their wellness goals.

My journey with yoga began as a personal quest for balance in a hectic world. What started as a practice became a passion, and eventually my purpose. I've trained in India's premier yoga institutes and blend traditional techniques with modern understanding of body mechanics.

My teaching philosophy centers on making yoga accessible to everyone, regardless of age, fitness level or experience. I believe yoga is not about perfect poses, but about connecting with yourself and finding peace in movement.
